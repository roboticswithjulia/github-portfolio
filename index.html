About Me

I am a Robotics Software Engineer. I have been working in different areas of robotics acquiring mechanical, electronics and programming skills. First, as a robotic teacher, giving classes to children and teenagers about robots and micro-controllers. Next, I started to work in the industry , developing and programming robots using modern robotics practices and technologies like ROS, computer vision AI and deep learning. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn, grow and endow robots with new capabilities.

Let's collaborate and bring robots to new levels!

Hardware integration

Developing custom software for different hardware components integrating sensors, actuators and micro-controllers in custom applications.

Simulation

Prototype and validate hardware designs through simulation software to assess technical feasibility, performance metrics, and potential challenges before physical implementation.

AI & Computer Vision

Implementing AI algorithms for computer vision, object detection, and machine learning to enhance robot capabilities and improve performance.
